TOOLS USED

PROJECT INFO

Client: Zulfah -Afriglobal Insurance brokers

MY ROLE

Product, UI/UX design & Research

THE GOAL

Afriglobal Insurance Brokers wants to make getting insurance easier for their clients. The aim is to allow users communicate faster and more efficiently with their insurance company . Our team was therefore tasked to work in the interest of both the users and brokers.

Solution

Puting the users first...

After forming our research findings, we came up with three divergent design ideas that tackled the problem space, yet still related back to the ultimate goal of improving the commuting experience for a city newcomer.

Understanding the user

Insurance users in Nigeria are generally within the middle-class and higher-class population. They are mostly office-workers with limited time as a major constraint.
